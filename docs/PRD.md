# Executive Summary

TOKYO Packaging Store is a specialized B2B & B2C e-commerce platform for disposable packaging products, restaurant supplies and paper products.

The platform allows restaurants, cafés, companies and individual customers to browse, compare and purchase packaging products quickly through a modern Arabic-first shopping experience.

The project focuses on simplicity, speed, scalability and long-term maintainability.

# Definition of Success

The project is considered successful when:

Customers can quickly find products.

Businesses can purchase packaging supplies in bulk.

The checkout process is completed in less than two minutes.

The interface feels professional, fast and trustworthy.

The architecture is scalable enough to support tens of thousands of products without major refactoring.

Every technical decision should prioritize maintainability, performance and user experience over unnecessary complexity.
